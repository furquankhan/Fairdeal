About Us

The Fairdeal Group of Companies is a fast growing family owned business. We are a company geared towards providing kenyans with a plethora of choices in the construction and real estate industries. With five dedicated divisions, Fairdeal Properties Ltd, Fairdeal Furniture Ltd, Fairdeal Glass Ltd, Fairdeal Glazing Ltd & Aqiq Trading Ltd our aim is to cater to any of our customer's needs by providing quality products at fair prices.


Our progression into the real estate industry stems from the experience of our sister companies dealing in the building material and finishing businesses. With several mega projects to our name, Fairdeal Properties is present in both Mombasa and Nairobi.

  • Over 2,000,000 sqft of construction in a period of 7 years
  • 500+ apartments completed and handed over
  • Developers of landmark buildings such as Epic Business Park and Fairdeal Plaza
  • Mega warehousing projects in Kikambala, Embakassi and Syokimau
  • 9 projects in Mombasa and 8 projects in Nairobi constructed or ongoing

OUR GUIDING PRINCIPLES


Sustainability through Customers

Our company's greatest asset is its customers, because without customers there is no company.

Relationships through Trust and Reliability

Providing real service means you must add something which cannot be bought or measured with money, and that is sincerity and integrity.

Growth and Advancement through Diversity

Learning happens through differences, not similarities. We encourage our people to challenge the status quo, offer solutions and modernize our businesses.

OUR MISSION

To delight customer by providing quality life spaces through continuous innovation.


OUR VISION

Ensure our customer's hard-earned money is transformed into their dream homes and offices.
